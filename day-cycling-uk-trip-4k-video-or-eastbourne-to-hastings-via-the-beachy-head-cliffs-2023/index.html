Day Cycling UK Trip 4K Video | Eastbourne to Hastings via The Beachy Head Cliffs 2023

Enjoy this relaxing first person bicycle ride view from a day trip cycle I took from the sunny seaside town of Eastbourne to Hastings seaside town, but my first stop was the gruelling 3 mile uphill ride to the famous Beachy Head cliffs, after getting off the train from London I had a bite to eat and got started straight away by heading down to Eastbourne beach and then tackling the Beachy Head route which I finally made it up there after the hill taking my breath away! Then I took the route all the way from Beachy Head through Eastbourne and onto Hastings finally arriving at the pier and ending my journey there! as ever, enjoy! :)
